# AI Daily — Landing Page Marketing Copy

**Document version:** 1.0
**Date:** April 26, 2026
**Author:** Marketing
**Voice:** Direct, plainspoken, useful. We sound like the smart coworker who already read the AI news so you don't have to. No hype, no jargon, no superlatives we can't back up with a source link.

---

## Brand voice cheat sheet

- **Action-first.** Lead with what the reader will do, not with what we are.
- **Concrete over abstract.** "Copy-paste prompts" beats "AI-powered productivity solutions."
- **Cite the source.** When we make a claim, we link it. This is part of the brand.
- **Time-respectful.** Headlines under 12 words. Body paragraphs under 40 words. Buttons under 4 words.
- **No filler.** Cut "We're excited to," "In today's fast-paced world," "Whether you're a…", and any sentence that could be deleted without losing meaning.

## 5. Feature deep-dive sections

Each feature gets a 60–80 word block on the marketing page, paired with a screenshot.

### 5.1 Unpack

> **Turn any article into things you can do today.**
>
> Paste an AI news link or article text. In about ten seconds, you get a one-line summary, a verified source card, and three to five tips with the prompt already written for you. Built for the moment your boss forwards a vendor blog post and asks "what does this mean for us?"

### 5.2 Advisor

> **Which AI tool should I use for this?**
>
> Describe a task in one line. We pick the best tool from your toolkit, give you a copy-paste prompt, and flag the tools you should avoid — with the tradeoff sources cited. The recommendation is constrained to the AIs you actually have, so you never see "Just buy GPT-4o Pro Max" as the answer.

### 5.3 Ask

> **Ask anything. Get prompts that actually work.**
>
> Describe a task or question. We search the live web, ground every tip in cited sources you can verify, and pull in optional YouTube walkthroughs when a video explains it better than text. This is what Google would be if Google handed you a copy-paste prompt instead of ten blue links.

### 5.4 Today

> **Six minutes with coffee. Three things you can try by 10 AM.**
>
> The Today feed publishes a fresh edition every morning while you sleep. Filter by category, search by keyword, save what you like. The personalization banner at the top shows what we tuned for — your role, your toolkit, your skill level — and one click changes any of it.

### 5.5 Library

> **Your AI playbook, ready to copy.**
>
> Tips you saved, organized so you can find the one that worked last Tuesday. We track time saved, project monthly savings if you reuse them weekly, and surface the tool you lean on most. Click any saved tip to open the full prompt and the original source.

## 8. FAQ section

Voice rule: question in the user's language, answer in plain English, max two short paragraphs.

**Q: Is this a newsletter?**
> No. It's a web product you visit. The daily feed publishes overnight, and you can browse, search, and save tips when it suits you. There's no email digest in this version.

**Q: Do you use my data to train AI?**
> No. Your saved tips and prompts stay in your account. We don't fine-tune any model on user content.

**Q: Which AI tools do you support?**
> ChatGPT Plus and Free, Microsoft Copilot Pro and Free, Google Gemini Advanced and Free, Claude Pro and Free, Perplexity Pro and Free, Kimi, Kimi Pro, DeepSeek, and Qwen. Tell us which ones you have during onboarding and Advisor will only recommend those.

**Q: Can I use this without signing up?**
> Yes. Browse the daily feed and run one Unpack, one Advisor question, and one Ask question per week without an account. Sign up free to lift the limits and save tips.

**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT?**
> ChatGPT is a tool. AI Daily is the morning briefing that tells you which tool to use and the prompt to paste. Think of us as the layer above your AI tools, not a replacement for any of them.

**Q: Where do the tips come from?**
> Official vendor blogs only — OpenAI, Microsoft, Google, Anthropic, Perplexity, Moonshot AI, Alibaba and DeepSeek. We link the source on every tip so you can verify.

**Q: How often does the feed update?**
> Once a day, overnight. The Today page is the same edition for everyone on a given calendar day. The on-demand tools (Unpack, Advisor, Ask) generate fresh results live every time you use them.

## 12. Words and phrases we don't use

Bans exist to keep the voice tight. If a draft contains any of these, rewrite.

- "Revolutionize" / "Revolutionary"
- "Game-changer" / "Game-changing"
- "Cutting-edge"
- "Unleash" / "Unlock" (when used metaphorically)
- "AI-powered" (we just say "AI")
- "Seamless" / "Seamlessly"
- "Best-in-class"
- "Whether you're a {x} or a {y}"
- "In today's fast-paced world"
- "We're excited to announce"
- Any sentence that opens with "Imagine if…"
